01 — Research Focus

Where mathematics meets intelligent systems

Four threads run through my research — from the deep-learning models that read sensor signals to the formal theory beneath computation itself.

/01

Human Activity Recognition

Deep learning on wearable & inertial sensor data to recognize daily, sport, and clinical activities in real-world settings.

/02

Deep Learning for Health

Residual and attention networks for fall detection, EEG seizure detection, and behavioural biometric authentication.

/03

IoT & Intelligent Sensing

Sensor fusion, edge models, and IoT systems for activity, mobility, and environment recognition at scale.

/04

Theory of Computation

Formal language theory, automata, and learnability — the mathematical foundations that underpin computing.
